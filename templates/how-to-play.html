To perform attack, you have to click once on your card and:

- twice on any of the opponent's cards

- once on the enemy avatar

To end your turn, just press the "End Turn" button, then the bot player will make a move.

More information about the special cards:

- There are 2 type of special cards: Health-stealing cards and Mana-stealing cards.

- After a special card is used, it becomes a normal one.
